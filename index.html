Characteristics of FCFS:

  • FCFS supports non-preemptive and preemptive CPU scheduling algorithm.
  • Tasks are always executed on a First-come, First-serve concept.
  • FCFS is easy to implement and use.
  • This algorithm is not much efficient in performance, and the wait time is quite high.

Characteristics of Non-Preemptive:

  • Resources are used and then held by the process until it gets terminated.
  • The process is not interrupted until its life cycle is complete.
  • Starvation can occur when a process with large burst time occupies the system.
  • No such overheads are required.

Characteristics of Preemptive:

  • Resources are allocated according to the cycles for a limited time.
  • The process can be interrupted, even before the completion.
  • Starvation may be caused, due to the insertion of priority process in the queue.
  • Maintaining queue and remaining time needs storage overhead.
